Features

Robust and long-lasting

The electric skateboard comes with a 25.2V 5.2Ah battery that suports long runtime. We haven't charged it since the project begin!

Clean circuit

Our circuit has a minimalist design. No abundant components and not a single outstanding wire. Everything is soldered perfectly thanks to my amazing girlfriend.

Super Fast

Our skateboard comes in 6 speed settings, capable of providing you with the most thrilling riding experience. Mom would never have to worry about you not getting to class on time!

Easy Navigation

Joseph is designed to be easy to learn and navigate. Our remote controller comes with a joystick for flexible speed contorl, and three buttons for setting cruise speed. Jump on and start your ride in no time.

Simple & fun display

The remote controller comes with a display showing the current speed so you you're always on top of how fast you're moving. We also included a tagline to remind people to "Stay slim".

Obstacle detection & warning

Our skateboard also comes with an ultrasonic sensor to detect obstacles in front. A "DANGER" sign shows up on the remote controller when an object is within 80cm of the skateboard to remind the user to take causion.
